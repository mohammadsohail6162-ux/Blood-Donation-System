About Blood Donation System

Our Blood Donation System is dedicated to saving lives by connecting donors with patients in need. We aim to make blood donation easy, safe, and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

To ensure timely and sufficient blood supply for patients, raise awareness about the importance of blood donation, and encourage more people to become voluntary donors.

Our Vision

To build a community where blood donation is a regular habit, saving lives every day and making our society healthier and stronger.

Blood Donation Process

Step 1: Registration

Register online or at the donation center with your personal details and blood type.

Step 2: Medical Checkup

Undergo a quick health checkup to ensure you are fit to donate blood.

Step 3: Donation

Donate blood safely under medical supervision.

Step 4: Refreshments

Enjoy some refreshments and rest for a few minutes after donation.

How to Donate

  • Be between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Weigh at least 50 kg.
  • Be healthy and free from infections.
  • Eat a light meal before donation.
  • Drink plenty of water before and after donation.
